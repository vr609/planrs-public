🌾 Indian farmers are using AI apps to protect their crops. What can these AI apps do?
✅ Correct! Farmers in Maharashtra and Punjab use apps where they take a photo of a sick-looking plant leaf, and AI identifies the disease and suggests treatment immediately!
Question 3 of 8
⚡ Which of these is a key BENEFIT of AI compared to humans doing the same work?
✅ Correct! AI never needs a chai break — it processes data millions of times faster than humans and can work 24 hours a day. However, AI is NOT always right and can make mistakes, especially with biased data!
Question 4 of 8
🤔 What is 'AI Bias'? Why is it a problem?
✅ Correct! If an AI is trained mostly on data from one group of people, it may work poorly or unfairly for others. For example, an AI for hiring that was trained on data from only one region might unfairly reject candidates from other backgrounds.
Question 5 of 8
🔒 Why is PRIVACY an important concern with AI?
✅ Correct! AI apps on your phone track your location, search history, shopping habits, and much more. This raises important questions about who owns this data and how it is used or protected.
Question 6 of 8
🎬 What is a 'Deepfake' — an AI risk everyone should know about?
✅ Correct! Deepfakes are AI-created videos where a real person's face or voice is replaced with false content. They can spread dangerous misinformation. This is why digital literacy and critical thinking are so important!
Question 7 of 8
💼 Which of these is a REAL career someone can have in the AI field?
✅ Correct! AI Ethicists are real professionals who study the social impact of AI, identify bias, and make sure AI systems treat all people fairly. Other real AI careers include ML Engineer, AI Designer, AI Researcher, and Data Scientist!
Question 8 of 8
🌟 The best way to think about AI as a tool is:
✅ Correct! AI is like a knife — it can cook delicious biryani or cause harm, depending on who uses it and how. It is a tool, and its positive or negative impact depends on the values and decisions of the humans who build and control it. That's why YOUR understanding of AI matters!
